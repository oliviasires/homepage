About

My homepage explores the idea of the butterfly effect through small, seemingly insignificant interactions that gradually lead to larger visual changes. Each action a user takes—whether clicking or navigating—contributes to a hidden system of growth that is only revealed over time. The site reflects how minor moments can accumulate and create unexpected outcomes, even when the initial action feels small or unnoticed. Through subtle shifts and evolving visuals, the experience becomes a space where cause and effect are not immediate, but layered and unfolding.
