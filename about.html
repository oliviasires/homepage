About

My homepage explores the concept of the Butterfly Effect—the idea that small actions can lead to significant and unexpected outcomes over time. Through gradual visual changes, evolving imagery, and layered interactions, the site reflects how seemingly insignificant moments can accumulate and transform an environment. Each page represents a stage of growth, where every click, navigation choice, or interaction contributes to a larger unfolding system. What begins as minimal and unnoticed slowly develops into something more complex, emphasizing that in connected systems, no action is ever truly small.
